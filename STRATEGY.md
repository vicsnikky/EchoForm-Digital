# SchoolPulse Product Strategy

## Vision Statement
To be the "Operating System" for African schools, starting with Nigeria. We aim to digitize every administrative touchpoint, reducing workload by 90% while increasing transparency for parents.

## Product Goals
1. **Efficiency**: Reduce result collation time from weeks to hours.
2. **Transparency**: Real-time attendance and fee visibility for parents.
3. **Accessibility**: 100% functional on low-end Android devices and 3G networks.
4. **Trust**: Secure, tamper-proof records for academic and financial data.

## MVP Definition
- **Multi-tenant Core**: School onboarding and student/teacher management.
- **Attendance**: QR-based and manual marking.
- **Results**: Score entry, automated collation, and PDF report cards.
- **Fees**: Basic invoice tracking and payment recording.
- **Library**: Document distribution for vetting workflows.

## User Personas
- **Super Admin**: School owners/principals focused on overview and approvals.
- **Teachers**: Busy professionals needing fast data entry.
- **Bursars**: Financial gatekeepers tracking every Naira.
- **Parents**: Information-hungry guardians on-the-go.
- **Gate Prefects**: On-the-ground attendance recorders.

## Success Metrics
- **Churn Rate**: Schools staying term-after-term.
- **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Proportion of teachers and students logged in dialy.
- **Collation Speed**: Average time from exam end to report card publishing.
- **Parent Engagement**: Number of result views per student.

## Monetization Strategy
- **SaaS Subscription**: ₦1,000 per student per term.
- **Premium Add-ons**: SMS notifications (Termii integration), custom branding.
- **Platform Fee**: Small convenience fee for future online fee payments.

## Competitive Advantage
- **Localized UX**: Built specifically for the Nigerian school structure (Terms, Sesions, Grades).
- **Data Economy**: Extremely lightweight pages (no heavy JS unless needed).
- **Offline Resilience**: Local caching for attendance marking.
